# CQECMPLX-Paper-Proof-Bundle

Status: `trackable-publish-candidate`

Route: `production/papers` + `production/proof-receipts`

## Composite Identity

`CQECMPLX-Paper-Proof-Bundle` is the text-first publication route for CQE
papers, formal layers, tool layers, workbook layers, intent indexes, and proof
receipts.

## Included Families

- `CQE-paper-00` through `CQE-paper-32`.
- `PAPER-BODY.md`.
- `SOURCE.md`.
- `01-CQE-formal/FORMAL.md`.
- `02-CQE-tool/TOOL.md` and `run.py` where present.
- `03-CQE-workbook/WORKBOOK.md`.
- paper intent index.
- proof receipt JSON files.

## Deferred Families

- PDF masters.
- complete paper kits.
- workbook PDFs.
- superseded PDFs.

## Production Rule

Promote text, formal, tool, workbook, and receipt material first. Binary PDF
artifacts enter only after size and lineage review.
