# CQECMPLX-TMN-Role-Family

Status: `trackable-profile-bound`

Route: `production/tool-families`

## Composite Identity

`CQECMPLX-TMN-Role-Family` tracks the named role/tool-family vocabulary visible
in TMN and TMN1 profiles.

## Named Signals

Examples include:

- CMPLX-Forge
- CMPLX-Intake
- CMPLX-Weaver
- CMPLX-Librarian
- CMPLX-Archivist
- CMPLX-Cartographer
- CMPLX-Navigator
- CMPLX-Sentinel
- CMPLX-Mirror
- CMPLX-Crucible

## Production Rule

Promote the role taxonomy before per-role implementation. A name becomes a
production tool family only after its source, adapter boundary, and CQE route are
bound.
