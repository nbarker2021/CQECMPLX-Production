## Use in Paper 6

Paper 6 generalizes the same discipline from objects to dependencies. Paper 3
registers one state across coordinate languages. Paper 6 registers one proof

```text

```

The Paper 3 receipt can therefore become a Paper 6 edge receipt, but the edge
validity is proved by Paper 6, not by Paper 3.

