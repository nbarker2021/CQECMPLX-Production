# Paper 21.50 - MorphForge Claim Contract

## Admission Rule

A Paper 21 claim is admitted only when it supplies a chosen observation event, a
finite ribbon or shell subtrajectory, a reversible word or replay record, a
morphon accounting row, and an explicit closure status. If the claim uses the
24-dimensional lattice suite, it must also say whether the landing is a template,
a verified construction, or an open bridge.

## Required Fields

Each admitted row must provide:

- source identifier,
- observer-selected centroid or object,
- local-window rule,
- encoded ribbon word or replay table,
- round-trip or replay check,
- morphon record,
- transform record,
- projection record,
- accounting link,
- closure status,
- failure or residue label if not closed,
- terminal template evidence if the row invokes the lattice package.

## Invalid Promotions

The following promotions are rejected by contract:

- lossless codec to universal token theory,
- `pass_with_open_gaps` to closed model,
- `Niemeier:E8^3` terminal template to completed Leech construction,
- SK-combinator narrative to formal proof,
- applied product sketch to scientific closure,
- domain claim without a domain verifier,
- analog reproducibility to experimental confirmation.

## Merkle-Style Carry

The Paper 21 contract may be linked into later papers as a chain item. The later
paper inherits the receipt hash, closure status, and open obligations. It may
not edit those obligations out; it can only discharge them with a stronger
receipt.

## Reviewer Test

A reviewer should ask one question first: can I replay the observation into the
same ribbon? If yes, the reviewer then asks whether the morphon ledger states
exactly what is closed and what remains open. If either answer is no, the claim
does not enter the CQE paper suite.
