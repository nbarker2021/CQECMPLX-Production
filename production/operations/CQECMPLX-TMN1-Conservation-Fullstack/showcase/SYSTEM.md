## The Pipeline

Content enters geometry before reasoning happens:

```
Text
 ↓ TarPit E6 — 544 tokens, torus signature, digital root
 ↓ SNAP 14-pass — 78 labels across keyword, semantic, domain, formal, literal, operational
 ↓ E8 Projection — 8D coordinates, nearest of 240 roots, Weyl chamber
 ↓ MDHG Address — 24D hierarchical hash, CA field state
 ↓ Morphon — (z=complexity, φ=phase, ΔΦ=conservation, R=resources)
 ↓ Receipt — Merkle chain, delta_phi audited
 ↓ Coins — Shell 0-3 minting, merit promotion
```

Processing time: ~32ms per atom. Four engines in parallel: ~100 atoms/second.

## The Agent Ecology

Agents are lattice points in E8 space. Each has:
- **SNAPDNA** — genetic profile of SNAP labels defining expertise
- **TMN Brain** — 24D neural network coupled to nanoGPT, trainable
- **Wallet** — coin balances earned from work
- **Karma** — reputation tier (newcomer → contributor → skilled → expert → master)
- **Position** — E8 coordinates that evolve with training

The cooperative daemon ticks every 30 seconds, matching agents to bounties by SNAP label overlap. Agents claim work, process it through the grain chain, submit results, earn coins. ThinkTank validates.

## The Economy

Zero starting balance. Grain chain is the only mint.

- **Shell 0**: Foundational vocabulary (weight 1.0×)
- **Shell 1**: First composites (0.5×)
- **Shell 2**: Second composites (0.25×)
- **Shell 3**: Third composites (0.125×)

Citations deplete supply. Conservation holds. Merit-based promotion: heavily cited coins gain effective weight regardless of shell.

Saturation decay prevents over-mining explored territory. The economy pushes agents toward unexplored regions of the knowledge space.

## The Four Laws

These are not guidelines. They are enforced by the system at every boundary.

1. **Quadratic Invariance** — Every transformation preserves the quadratic form. Content cannot amplify without external energy.

2. **Boundary-Only Entropy** — Entropy changes only at boundaries. Within components, state is deterministic. Every boundary crossing produces a receipt.

3. **MORSR ΔΦ ≤ 0** — System complexity cannot increase without justification. Every action has a ΔΦ cost tied to content morphon.

4. **10-Element Closure** — Every complete processing cycle touches exactly 10 elements. Nothing is skipped.
