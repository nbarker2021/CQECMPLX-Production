# CMPLX-MORSR Umbrella Contract

CMPLX-MORSR is the CMPLX diagnostic spine for reading shape from the middle out.

It does not start from a boundary. It starts from a C centroid, pulses the E8 field, records what instantly activates, confirms one reading, and recenters. Three recenterings form the confirmed extension of the first centroid.

## Identity

CMPLX-MORSR names the entire diagnostic identity, not a single algorithm. Anything placed under this umbrella must obey the same method, schema, receipts, hidden-ablation rule, and port shape.

## Scope

CMPLX-MORSR applies to:

- runtime diagnostics
- module health scans
- port mismatch detection
- error/failure/success point discovery
- active-node and shadow-node discovery
- cross-component route discovery
- non-math validation and review

CMPLX-MORSR does not replace proof-grade math verification. Math verification can expose its expected result when the proof requires it. Non-math action, diagnostic, recommendation, classification, and validation must hide the expected result until after the guess.

## Inheritance Rule

Every CMPLX-MORSR component must keep the Forge-family shape:

- named identity umbrella
- manifest/state/summary
- engine contract
- schemas
- diagnostic receipts
- open obligations
- review and repair loop
- honesty ablation layer

No CMPLX-MORSR tool is allowed to be a loose script without a manifest and trace.
