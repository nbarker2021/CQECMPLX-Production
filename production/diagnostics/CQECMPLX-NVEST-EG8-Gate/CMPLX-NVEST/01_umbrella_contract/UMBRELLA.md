# CMPLX-NVEST Umbrella Contract

CMPLX-NVEST names the whole investigate/NVest diagnostic identity.

It receives an applied place: a market, product, engine, workflow, dataset, network, model run, diagnostic trace, or any signal-bearing system. It decomposes that place into waves, centroids, regimes, active points, and gates.

## Scope

CMPLX-NVEST applies to:

- market and financial signals
- product telemetry
- model/tool diagnostics
- workflow health
- network events
- failure/success localization
- phase-shift detection
- centroid tension measurement
- value/risk estimation

## Rule

Do not treat NVEST as a trading-only tool. Trading is one product surface. The underlying tool is a wave/centroid diagnostic sniffer.

## Pairing

CMPLX-NVEST pairs with `CQE-E/G8`.

- CMPLX-NVEST investigates and applies.
- CQE-E/G8 validates, gates, and binds to formal/library structure.
