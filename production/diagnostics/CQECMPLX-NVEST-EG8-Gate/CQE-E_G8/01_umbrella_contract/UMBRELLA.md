# CQE-E/G8 Umbrella Contract

CQE-E/G8 is the CQE gate identity for decomposed investigations.

It receives candidates from an applied investigation surface, especially CMPLX-NVEST, and decides what passes through the E8/G8 gate.

## Scope

- gate wave candidates
- validate estimator choices
- score hidden-result predictions
- classify phase/regime decisions
- bind accepted results to library records
- reject contaminated or leaked diagnostics

## Rule

Every non-math gate decision commits before reveal.
